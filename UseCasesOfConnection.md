Main Uses
1. Page Synchronization
Syncs your WordPress pages to the Siloq platform for SEO analysis
Automatic sync when you publish or update pages
Bulk sync all pages at once

2. AI Content Generation
Generate SEO-optimized content using AI
Import AI-generated content as drafts or replace existing content
Automatic FAQ section generation

3. Schema Markup (Structured Data)
Automatically injects JSON-LD schema markup into your pages
Helps Google understand your content better
Improves chances of appearing in rich search results

4. SEO Optimization
Syncs meta titles, descriptions, and keywords
Internal linking suggestions
Content silo management (organizing related content)

5. Webhook Integration
Real-time notifications when content is generated
Instant updates when schema is updated
